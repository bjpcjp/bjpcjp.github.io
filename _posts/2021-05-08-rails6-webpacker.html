Rails 6 with Webpacker startup issues

Just a quick note - anytime you create a Rails v6 application, be sure to 1) upgrade your Webpacker gem from 4.x to (at least) 5.3, then 2) run rails webpacker:install afterwards.

This is to avoid a missing manifest.json error that appears when you attempt to view a new scaffold.
