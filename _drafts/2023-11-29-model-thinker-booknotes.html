This is a book about models. It describes dozens of models in straightforward language and explains how to apply them. It promotes a many-model thinking approach - the application of ensembles of models to make sense of complex phenomena.

The models we cover fall into three classes - simplifications of the world, mathematical analogies, and exploratory, artificial constructs.
