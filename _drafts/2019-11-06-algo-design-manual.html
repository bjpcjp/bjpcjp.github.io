Growth Rates

  • Constant: f(n) = 1
  • Logarithmic: f(n) = log(n)
  • Linear: f(n) = n
  • Superlinear: f(n) = n * log(n)
  • Quadratic: f(n) = n^2
  • Cubic: f(n) = n^3
  • Exponential: f(n) = c^n
  • Factorial: f(n) = n!
